BMI Calculator



About this calculator:

This BMI calculator is a simple web application that allows users to calculate their Body Mass Index (BMI) based on their weight and height. It provides a quick and easy way to assess whether an individual has a healthy body weight for a given height.

BMI vs Fitness

BMI > 25: Overweight

BMI < 18.5: Underweight

BMI between 18.5 and 24.9: Normal weight
